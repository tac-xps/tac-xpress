Welcome to Tac-Xpress
Delivering Trust Across Distance
At Tac-Xpress, we believe that every shipment carries more than just goods—it carries trust, expectation, and a promise. For over 15 years, we have been serving as a dependable bridge between Imphal and New Delhi, connecting people, businesses, and communities through reliable cargo services.

Founded out of necessity and shaped by experience, Tac-Xpress began as a response to the unique logistical challenges faced by the Northeast region of India. Over time, what started as a modest operation has grown into a trusted cargo partner for thousands of individuals and businesses.

We are not just in the business of transportation—we are in the business of delivering certainty, care, and continuity.

Our Story
Built Through Challenge, Driven by Purpose
The Northeast region of India presents a landscape unlike any other—geographically, culturally, and logistically. For years, individuals and businesses faced significant barriers in moving goods efficiently to and from major metropolitan hubs like New Delhi.

Tac-Xpress was founded to address this gap.

What began as a necessity soon became a mission. Navigating difficult terrain, unpredictable conditions, and limited infrastructure, we committed ourselves to creating a dependable cargo network that would serve the people of the region with integrity and consistency.

The journey was not without obstacles. From operational disruptions to systemic challenges, we encountered setbacks that tested our resilience. However, each challenge became an opportunity to learn, adapt, and improve.

We do not claim perfection. Instead, we take pride in our willingness to grow, to listen, and to evolve alongside our customers.

Today, Tac-Xpress stands as a testament to perseverance—built on trust, strengthened by experience, and guided by a clear sense of purpose.

Our Services
Comprehensive Cargo Solutions Tailored to Your Needs
At Tac-Xpress, we offer a range of logistics solutions designed to meet diverse requirements with efficiency and reliability. Each service is carefully structured to ensure that your shipment reaches its destination safely and on time.

Air Cargo
When speed is essential, our air cargo service provides a fast and secure solution for transporting goods between Imphal and New Delhi. We work with established airline networks to ensure timely dispatch and delivery, maintaining strict handling standards throughout the process.

From urgent documents to high-value shipments, our air cargo service is designed to deliver with precision and care.

Surface Cargo
For cost-effective and dependable transportation, our surface cargo service offers a reliable alternative. With a well-managed ground network, we ensure consistent movement of goods across routes, even in challenging conditions.

Our surface logistics operations are optimized for efficiency, allowing us to provide competitive pricing without compromising on service quality.

Pick-Up and Delivery
Convenience is at the core of our operations. Our pick-up and delivery service ensures that your shipment is handled from door to door, minimizing effort and maximizing efficiency.

Whether you are a business managing regular shipments or an individual sending personal items, our team coordinates every step to ensure a smooth experience.

Packaging Solutions
We understand that safe delivery begins with proper packaging. Our packaging services are designed to protect your goods throughout the journey, regardless of distance or mode of transport.

Using industry-standard materials and techniques, we ensure that each package is secured with care and attention to detail.
